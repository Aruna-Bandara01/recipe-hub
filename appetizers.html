Two Simple Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Prep: 10 min   |   Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette (sliced into 12 pieces)
  • 2 large ripe tomatoes (diced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: balsamic glaze for drizzling

Steps

  1. Toast baguette slices in a toaster or oven until lightly golden.
  2. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, minced garlic, chopped basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spoon the tomato mixture onto each toasted baguette slice.
  4. Drizzle a little balsamic glaze if desired and serve immediately.

Cheesy Stuffed Mushrooms

Prep: 15 min   |   Cook: 15 min   |   Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 12 large white mushrooms (stems removed)
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 small garlic clove (minced)
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a small baking dish or line with parchment.
  2. In a bowl, mix cream cheese, Parmesan, minced garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  3. Fill each mushroom cap with a spoonful of the cheese mixture.
  4. Place stuffed mushrooms in the baking dish, drizzle with a little olive oil.
  5. Bake for about 12–15 minutes, until mushrooms are tender and tops are lightly golden. Serve warm.
